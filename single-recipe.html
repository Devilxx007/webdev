prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Snacks Dinner

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on the pizza dough package or at 450°F (230°C) if using homemade dough. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface into your desired thickness. Transfer the dough onto a baking sheet or pizza stone.

step 2

Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Add your desired toppings on top of the cheese..

step 3

Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Slice and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade pizza!

ingredients

1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)

1/2 cup pizza sauce

2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
